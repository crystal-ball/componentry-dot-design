- The text component will render a `p` element
- The default variant is `body`, and the library has styles for `lead` and `caption`
  variants.
- Lead paragraphs are higher prominence and are typically used once to lead in a page of
  text.
- Captions are lower prominence and should be used for adding descriptions or secondary
  text content.
